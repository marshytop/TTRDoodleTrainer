## ⚙️ What This Program Does

This tool automates clicking specific points on your screen to repeatedly train doodles in ToonTown Rewritten.

It allows you to:
- Record click positions
- Loop those clicks with randomized timing
- Run continuously (AFK)

---

## ▶️ How to Use

### Step 1 — Record Positions
- Press **F5**
- Click **16 points** in the order required by the game

---

### Step 2 — Start Training
- Press **F6**
- The program will begin looping through your recorded clicks

---

### Step 3 — Stop
- Press **F9** to stop the loop at any time

---

### Step 4 — Minimize
- Press **F8** to minimize the window

---

## 🧠 Tips

- Keep your ToonTown window in the **same position**
- Do **not move or resize** the game window after recording clicks
- Make sure the clicks align properly before leaving it AFK
